Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Data Sovereignty

Your code is your intellectual property. We provide the tools; you retain the control. Our architecture is engineered to ensure your data never leaves your sight.

Zero-Knowledge Policy

We operate on a strict Zero-Knowledge architecture. Your source code, API keys, and environment variables are never transmitted to or stored on our servers. We have no backend database for user content, ensuring absolute confidentiality.

Local Storage Protocol

All project data is managed via our 'Local Storage Protocol'. Using industry-standard technologies like IndexedDB and LocalStorage, your files exist solely within your device's secure sandboxed environment. You own the hardware; you own the data.

Encryption & Safety

We implement rigorous safety measures, including the complete exclusion of sensitive fields from telemetry. Any performance metrics collected are anonymized and scrubbed of identifiers, while all AI communication utilizes enterprise-grade TLS 1.3 encryption.

1. Subscription Privacy

Pro subscriptions are handled entirely through the Apple App Store infrastructure. Web IDE does not store your credit card information, billing address, or purchase history. Your status is verified via secure, anonymized receipts issued by Apple.

2. Third-Party AI Providers

AI features are powered by industry-standard providers. Our enterprise-tier agreements ensure that any code transmitted for transient processing is never used for training public models and is purged immediately after the response is generated.

3. Children's Privacy

By design, Web IDE does not collect personal information, emails, or user profiles. This makes the application inherently safe for all age groups. we do not knowingly target or monitor data from users under the age of 13.
